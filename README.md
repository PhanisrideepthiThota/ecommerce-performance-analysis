# 📊 E-Commerce Business Performance Analysis (Case Study)

## 🎯 Objective

To analyse e-commerce business performance using profit trends, customer behaviour, and sales target comparisons in order to derive meaningful business insights.
This case study demonstrates how data analysis can be used to evaluate business performance and support decision-making.

---

## 📂 Project Structure

* **data/** → Dataset files (orders, order details, sales targets)
* **notebooks/** → Jupyter notebook containing full analysis
* **src/** → Python scripts for processing

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 🔹 1. Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Merged datasets using **Order ID**
* Converted date columns and extracted **Month**
* Structured data for analysis

---

### 🔹 2. Profit Trend Analysis

* Analysed monthly profit trends
* Identified fluctuations in business performance

---

### 🔹 3. Category-wise Analysis

* Evaluated performance across product categories
* Identified high-performing and low-performing categories

---

### 🔹 4. Customer Analysis

* Identified top customers contributing to profit
* Analysed customer concentration

---

### 🔹 5. Sales Target Analysis

* Compared actual profit with sales targets
* Identified gaps in performance

---

### 🔹 6. Correlation Analysis

* Analysed relationships between **Amount, Profit, and Quantity**

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Profit varies across months, showing inconsistent performance
* A small group of customers contributes a large share of profit
* Certain categories dominate overall profitability
* The business consistently fails to meet sales targets
* Negative profit periods indicate potential losses
* Strong relationship between revenue and profit

## 🚀 Conclusion

The analysis shows that the business is currently underperforming relative to its sales targets. Profitability is driven by a limited number of customers and categories, while overall performance remains inconsistent.

To improve performance, the business should:

* Focus on high-performing regions and categories
* Strengthen customer retention strategies
* Optimise pricing and operational efficiency
* Use data-driven decision-making for growth
